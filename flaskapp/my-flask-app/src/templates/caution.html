Important Notice

Please read these important notices before using the Result Analyzer

Data Accuracy Notice

Due to variations in PDF structure and formatting across different result sheets, some data extraction may be incomplete or inaccurate. Always verify the analyzed results with original documents.

Data Privacy

Your uploaded files are processed locally and deleted immediately after analysis. We do not store any personal information or result data on our servers.

PDF Compatibility

Tool works best with official MSBTE result PDFs. Modified or non-standard formatted PDFs may not be processed correctly. Please use original result sheets.

How Data Processing Works

1

PDF Upload

Your PDF is securely uploaded and processed locally on the server

2

Data Extraction

Text is extracted using pattern matching to identify student records, marks, and results

3

Analysis

Data is processed to calculate statistics, identify toppers, and generate insights

4

Verification Needed

Results should be cross-verified with original documents for accuracy

Known Limitations

  • May struggle with scanned PDFs
  • Complex subject names might be truncated
  • Special characters may cause issues
  • Very large files may take longer to process

Best Practices

  • Use original MSBTE result PDFs
  • Verify important statistics manually
  • Report any discrepancies found
  • Keep original documents for reference
